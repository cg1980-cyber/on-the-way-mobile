On the Way

Package tracking for households — currently in free beta.

⬇ Download for Android

Android only for now · version 1.2.0 · ~60 MB

How to install

  1. Tap Download for Android above. When it finishes, open the downloaded file.
  2. Android will warn that it's from an "unknown source" — tap Settings and allow installs from your browser, then tap Install. (This is normal for apps not yet on the Play Store.)
  3. Open On the Way and tap Sign Up.
  4. Sign up with the same email address your invite was sent to — you'll be dropped straight into the household.
  5. Allow notifications when asked, so you get delivery alerts.
Important: sign up with the exact email your invite came to. If you use a different email, the app won't know you were invited (you can still join later with the invite code from your email).

On an iPhone? On the Way isn't available for iOS yet — hang tight.
