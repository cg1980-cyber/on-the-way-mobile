Privacy Policy

On the Way — package tracking for households
Effective date: July 6, 2026

The short version: We don't read your email inbox. We don't show ads. We don't sell your data — to anyone, ever. We store only what the app needs to track your household's packages, and you can delete all of it yourself, in the app, at any time.

Who we are

On the Way is a package-tracking app for households, operated independently. Contact: support@onthewayapp.net.

What we collect

  • Account information. Your email address and a password (stored as a secure hash by our authentication provider). Optionally, your home address if you choose to provide it.
  • Forwarded shipping emails. The app gives you a unique forwarding address (e.g. yourname.abc123@onthewayapp.net). Shipping notifications sent to that address are parsed to extract package details — carrier, tracking number, merchant, status, and delivery estimate. We never connect to or scan your email inbox. Only mail you actively route to your forwarding address reaches us, and non-shipping emails are discarded rather than stored.
  • Package details you add. Nicknames, notes, recipient assignments, and gift-mode settings you enter in the app.
  • Household information. Household name, member display names, and invitation email addresses.
  • Push notification token. A device token so we can send package alerts, if you allow notifications.

We collect no analytics, advertising identifiers, location data, contacts, or browsing history. There are no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs in the app.

How we use it

  • To show your household's packages in one shared feed.
  • To send package alerts (new package, out for delivery, delivered) to household members' devices.
  • To look up live tracking status with carriers via our tracking-data processor (tracking numbers only).
  • To send household invitation emails you request.
  • To respond when you contact support.

That's the complete list. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for advertising or marketing, and we do not use it to build profiles.

Household sharing — what members can see

On the Way is household-first by design: members of your household see the household's shared package feed, including packages addressed to other members. Two exceptions:

  • Gift mode lets you hide a specific package from a specific member (so surprises stay surprises).
  • Packages you had before joining a household move with you and leave with you.

Only join a household with people you trust to see your deliveries.

Service providers (processors)

We use a small set of infrastructure providers to run the service. Each receives only what it needs to do its job:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (account, packages, household data).
  • Railway — hosts our backend server.
  • Cloudflare — routes email sent to your forwarding address.
  • Brevo — delivers invitation and support emails.
  • Expo & Google Firebase Cloud Messaging — deliver push notifications to your device.
  • EasyPost — live tracking-status lookups (receives tracking numbers only, no personal details).

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and stored on servers located in the United States. Database access is restricted with row-level security so each household can only reach its own data.

Deleting your data

In the app: Settings → Delete my account and data. This permanently deletes your account, your packages, your push tokens, and your household membership (ownership of a shared household passes to its longest-standing member; a household with no remaining members is deleted).

By email: if you can't access the app, send a deletion request to support@onthewayapp.net from your account email and we'll complete it within 30 days.

Data retention

Your data is kept while your account is active and deleted when you delete your account. Forwarded emails that aren't shipping notifications are not stored at all.

Children

On the Way is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update this page and note it in the app. The effective date above always reflects the current version.

Contact
