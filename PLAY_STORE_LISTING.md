## Full description

**One feed for the whole household.**
On the Way is the package tracker built for households, not just individuals.
Everyone at your address sees every package in one shared feed — who it's
for, where it is, and when it arrives.

**Why households love it**
• 📦 Shared feed — every member sees the household's incoming packages
• 👤 Per-person filters — see just Cory's packages, or everyone's
• 🎁 Gift mode — hide a surprise from one person so birthdays stay secret
• 🔔 Alerts for whoever's home — delivery notifications go to the whole
  household, not just the buyer
• 🚚 Live tracking status across USPS, UPS, FedEx, and more
• ✍️ Notes and nicknames so "SHIP-8829-XJ" becomes "Dad's birthday shoes"

**Private by design**
• No inbox scanning — you forward shipping emails to your own private
  tracking address; we never connect to your email account
• No ads, no data selling, no tracking SDKs
• Delete your account and all your data in-app, any time

**Free during beta**
On the Way is in open beta and completely free while we build. No ads, no
paywall — just honest package tracking while we polish the experience.
